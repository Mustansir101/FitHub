Exercise Plans

Discover targeted workouts for every muscle group

Shoulders

Key Exercises

  • Overhead Press

    Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, press the barbell or dumbbells overhead. 3 sets of 8-12 reps.

  • Lateral Raises

    Hold dumbbells at your sides, raise them laterally until arms are parallel to the floor. 3 sets of 12-15 reps.

  • Face Pulls

    Using a cable machine, pull the rope attachment toward your face, focusing on rear deltoids. 3 sets of 15 reps.

Chest

Key Exercises

  • Bench Press

    Lie on a bench, lower the barbell to your chest and press back up. 4 sets of 8-10 reps.

  • Incline Dumbbell Press

    On an inclined bench, press dumbbells upward, targeting upper chest. 3 sets of 10-12 reps.

  • Push-Ups

    Classic bodyweight exercise targeting chest, shoulders, and triceps. 3 sets of 15-20 reps.

Biceps/Triceps

Key Exercises

  • Bicep Curls

    Stand with dumbbells, curl them toward your shoulders. 3 sets of 12 reps.

  • Hammer Curls

    Similar to bicep curls but with palms facing each other. 3 sets of 12 reps.

  • Tricep Dips

    Using parallel bars or a bench, lower your body by bending your elbows. 3 sets of 12-15 reps.

  • Tricep Pushdowns

    Using a cable machine, push the bar down by extending your elbows. 3 sets of 12-15 reps.

Back

Key Exercises

  • Pull-Ups

    Hang from a bar and pull your body up until your chin is over the bar. 3 sets of 8-12 reps.

  • Bent-Over Rows

    Bend at the hips, keep your back straight, and pull a barbell or dumbbells to your lower chest. 3 sets of 10-12 reps.

  • Lat Pulldowns

    Using a cable machine, pull the bar down to your chest. 3 sets of 12 reps.

Legs

Key Exercises

  • Squats

    Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, lower your body by bending your knees. 4 sets of 10-12 reps.

  • Lunges

    Step forward with one leg, lowering your body until both knees are bent at 90 degrees. 3 sets of 12 reps per leg.

  • Leg Press

    Using a leg press machine, push the platform away by extending your legs. 3 sets of 12 reps.

  • Calf Raises

    Stand on the edge of a step, raise your heels as high as possible. 3 sets of 15-20 reps.

Cardio

Key Exercises

  • Running

    30 minutes of steady-state running or interval training for improved cardiovascular health.

  • Cycling

    20-30 minutes of cycling, either outdoors or on a stationary bike. Great low-impact cardio option.

  • Jump Rope

    15 minutes of jump rope intervals. Excellent for coordination and calorie burning.

  • HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)

    20 minutes of alternating between high-intensity exercises and short rest periods.
